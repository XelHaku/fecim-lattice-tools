## Part 7: Ferroelectric CIM Positioning

### Unique Value Proposition

| Competitor Approach | Ferroelectric CIM Differentiator |
|--------------------|---------------------------|
| FMC: DRAM replacement | Compute-in-Memory (neural networks) |
| IBM: PCM-based | HfO₂/ZrO₂ superlattice (CMOS compatible) |
| Intel: Digital neuromorphic | True analog computation |
| Samsung: 3D NAND replacement | 30 analog states for AI weights |
| Startups: Various NVM | Dr. Tour's Flash Joule Heating IP |

### Competitive Advantages
1. **CMOS Compatibility:** Uses existing fab infrastructure
2. **Multi-level Cells:** 30 states vs 2-4 typical
3. **Dr. Tour's IP:** Flash Joule Heating synthesis
4. **external research institution Pipeline:** Strong academic collaboration
5. **Full Stack:** Materials + devices + system demos

### Competitive Risks
1. FMC has 2+ year head start and €100M funding
2. Samsung's Nature publication shows similar 5-bit cells
3. IBM's 64-core demo is production-ready
4. Intel Loihi has 200+ research project ecosystem
